Game Overview

Developed by Beat Games and released in 2018, Beat Saber is a virtual reality rhythm game where players slash colored blocks representing musical beats with paired lightsabers.

Key Features:

  • Official music packs from artists like Billie Eilish and BTS
  • Custom song support through mods
  • Single-player and party modes
